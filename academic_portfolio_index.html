About

I study how chemical structure, exposure, and biological response can be connected to improve hazard assessment of environmental contaminants.

My background spans marine environmental science, ecotoxicological testing, image-based embryo analysis, and computational toxicity prediction. I am especially interested in building interpretable approaches that connect QSAR-derived baseline toxicity predictions with observed organism-level effects and mechanism-of-action hypotheses.

Research Themes

A concise overview of my main scientific interests and methods.

QSAR & Baseline Toxicity

Zebrafish Embryo Toxicity
